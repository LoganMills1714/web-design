Passwords

Passwords are an integral part of cybersecurity. Passwords are used to protect sensitive information and are used as a form of authentication to access information. It's important to use strong passwords that you can remember or you could opt to use a password manager. Passphrases are passwords that have a string of words with numbers and symbols mixed in and are often easier to remember than the randomly generated passwords that password managers can generate for you.
